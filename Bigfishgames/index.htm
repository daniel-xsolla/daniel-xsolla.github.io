Take it from me folks, this is a really fun Match 3 game the entire family can enjoy. Atlantic's favorite heroes have returned to rebuild an underwater oasis, which can be played in all three types of Match 3 games. You can even switch back and forth between the traditional Swap Match 3, Chain or Group Match 3 where you click and drag horizontally, vertically and diagonally, or click on groups of 3 or more identical tiles.
The goal is to make matches over all Sand covered tiles and heal all fish that are inside bubbles by matching those tiles. You can earn up to 3 stars depending on the time it takes you to complete the level goals. You earn pearls for each level that is used to purchase new houses, Fisherman's Huts, Forges and even Farms. Some of the buildings you purchased have a Mini Game available when you click on them such as locating 10 hidden fish, or finding fragments to assemble a power-up bonus.
Some sand tiles are covered in Moss, requiring two matches on them, and as you progress, there are Oil filled tiles and Lava tiles that will spread or multiply if you don't make matches on them quickly. There are the usual obstructed tiles with Chains, Stones, and Crates, which require matches made next to them or matches made of the same item trapped inside.
When you make a match of 4 or more Tentacles, you will earn an Octopus Bomb which will explode when clicked, to destroy surrounding tiles. Other power-ups are unlocked when you collect their symbols by dropping them off of the board. The Hammerhead can destroy a single tile of your choice, the Bow shoots an arrow to destroy an entire row, Blowfish Bombs destroy all tiles in a square area, and the Laser Gun shoots lightning bolts to destroy random tiles.
Fish in Bubbles are replaced occasionally wit Hard Cases that need special elements to heal them by matching 4 objects inside orange bubbles, Zombie Fish are aggressive fish that will need to be released quickly or they will pollute the water, and then there are the Major Problem Fish that require several Match 4's to heal them.
This is one of those games that you'll want to play over and over, and with 96 available levels, it should be a decent length, enjoy!Show less
I recommend this game!

A new Match 3 game is always a treat so after playing the demo and 'enjoying' the challenge I decided to take advantage of yesterdays' Half price sale.
I found the game really difficult to achieve more than one of the possible three stars. Other than exploding octopus bombs, gained by matching four of them, no other power up is available until the Forge has been built and then a Hammer allows you to take out one square or obstacle.
After that an Arrow tool takes out one line, a Puffer fish bomb takes out a goodly section and a Laser takes out a column and a row simultaneously.
All power ups are limited to the three which are achieved by dropping their icons off the field by matching below them. There is no chance to gain any more by four matches etc.
I finished this game to the best of my abilities after a few hours play.
At the end is the sad confession from the devs that they simply ran out of ideas for more buildings so there is no point in continuing for me.
The graphics are brilliantly done although mega tiny and the music was boring orchestral more like a film theme.
The option to be able to change game style during the game in progress was a bonus but I am not a fan of either chain or collapse so never really tried them.
All in all a game to pass the time but I personally prefer more rewards for five matches of anything, more power ups and then a chance to upgrade buildings more than once.
Put in some more variety, bigger graphics and a better soundtrack and it would be better.Show less
I don't recommend this game.
